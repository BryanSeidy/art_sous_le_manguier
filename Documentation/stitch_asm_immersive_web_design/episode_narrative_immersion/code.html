Episode 04 — Series 1

The Hand that Carves the Wind

featuring Artist Malick Diop

In the shade of a century-old mango tree in Saint-Louis, Malick Diop explains how the grain of the wood dictates the soul of the mask. This episode explores the thin line between craftsmanship and spiritual medium, tracing the lineage of carving from ancestral relics to contemporary ethno-futurist installations.

We witness the rhythmic strike of the chisel, a sound that Malick calls "the heartbeat of the earth." Through long, meditative takes, the camera captures the transformation of raw mahogany into a vessel of cultural memory.
